About BuzzPi

BuzzPi is a web-based buzzing system designed for competitions and team games. It allows multiple teams to compete by buzzing in their answers, with precise timing and order tracking.

Built with Django and WebSocket technology, BuzzPi enables real-time interaction in offline environments, making it perfect for youth camps, game shows, and other team-based activities.
